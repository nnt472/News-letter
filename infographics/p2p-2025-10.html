Objective & Overview

Legal Framework

Decree 94/2025/ND-CP issued on 29 Apr 2025, effective 01 Jul 2025.

  • Sandbox period: 2 years + one possible extension.
  • Supervised by State Bank of Vietnam (SBV).
  • Mandatory incident and progress reports to SBV.
  • Focus areas: P2P lending, e-KYC, credit scoring, and data analytics.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Vietnam-registered legal entity.
  • Proven expertise in finance, IT, and risk management.
  • Solution demonstrates innovation and social impact.
  • Scalable potential with controlled risk exposure.

Key Requirements

Legal & Operational

  • Approved sandbox scope with participant caps.
  • No fund pooling or lending on the platform‚Äôs own balance sheet.
  • Consumer disclosure: rates, risks, and dispute mechanisms.
  • Clear exit route: Graduation ¬∑ Transformation ¬∑ Soft Exit.

Technology & Compliance

  • Full e-KYC & AML verification with audit trails.
  • Data must remain within Vietnam; encrypted storage & transit.
  • Independent security test prior to go-live.
  • Custodian integration with daily reconciliation.

Reporting & Oversight

  • Notify incidents to SBV within 24h, full report in 3 days.
  • Quarterly risk review; KPI reports (loans, defaults, users).
  • Annual system certification & renewal.

Cybersecurity Risk Controls

Baseline Security

  • ISO/IEC 27001:2019 ISMS and 27002:2020 controls.
  • Network segmentation, MFA, API gateways.
  • Data masking, tokenization, PDPD compliance.
  • Regular penetration testing and vendor audits.

Common Weaknesses

  • Commingling of investor funds in platform wallet.
  • Weak encryption or shared cloud credentials.
  • Over-reliance on third-party APIs without due diligence.
  • Delayed breach reporting to regulators.

Recommendations for Vietnam

  • Maintain the Information Intermediary structure for risk separation.
  • Develop a Cybersecurity & IT Audit Standard dedicated to sandbox firms.
  • Enable controlled linkages with custodian banks for fund segregation.
  • Introduce a post-sandbox roadmap with ‚Äúgraduate or exit‚Äù options.
  • Establish a joint regulator-industry Fintech Oversight Committee.
